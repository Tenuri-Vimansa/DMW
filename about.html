Our Services

X-Ray Facilities

Our hospital is equipped with high-resolution digital X-Ray machines, enabling quick and accurate imaging for bones, chest, and soft tissues. We ensure minimal radiation exposure while providing detailed diagnostic results for both emergency and routine examinations

Scan Facilities

We provide advanced CT and MRI scanning services for precise internal imaging. Our trained radiologists interpret scans promptly, aiding early detection and accurate treatment planning for conditions ranging from neurological disorders to complex internal injuries.

Consultation Facilities

Patients have access to experienced consultants across multiple specialties. Each consultation involves a thorough assessment of medical history, diagnostic reports, and personalized advice to ensure the most effective and patient-centered treatment plan.

Operation Facilities

Our modern operation theatres are equipped with state-of-the-art surgical instruments, anesthesia systems, and sterile environments. With a dedicated surgical team, we ensure safe, precise, and efficient procedures for a wide range of medical and emergency surgeries.
